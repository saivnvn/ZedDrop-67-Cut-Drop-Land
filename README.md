## 🎮 About the Game

**ZedDrop: Cut, Drop & Land Swing** is a high-speed precision game where timing is everything.

▶ Time the swing. Cut the rope. Control your fall.  
▶ Land perfectly… or lose everything.

It sounds simple — but one mistimed cut can destroy your entire combo.

---

## ⚡ Core Gameplay

▷ Cut too early → Miss  
▷ Cut too late → Fail  
▷ Cut perfectly → 💥 **Perfect Drop**

● Explosive effects  
● Smooth, satisfying landings  
● High-score bonuses  

---

## 🧠 Skill-Based Design

Easy to start. Brutal to master.

▶ Tap to cut, drop, and land  
▶ Master perfect timing  
▶ Chain combos with Perfect hits  
▶ Score-based difficulty scaling  
▶ Dynamic bars & obstacles every run  

---

## 🔥 Game Feel

Short runs. High tension. Pure skill.

▶ Climb the leaderboard  
▶ Beat your best score  
▶ Prove your precision  

**One more run? You already know the answer.**
